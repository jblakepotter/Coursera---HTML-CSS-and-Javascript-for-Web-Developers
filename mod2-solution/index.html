The Triforce

The Golden Power

Power

The triforce of power is the segment of triforce associated with the goddess of power, Din, credited flaming arms to cultivate the land of Hyrule.

Wisdom

The triforce of wisdom is the segment of triforce associated with the goddess of wisdom, Nayru, credited with using her power to create the physical laws of the realm of Hyrule.

Courage

The triforce of courage is the segment of triforce associated with the goddess of courage, Farore, credited with using her strength to create all living life on the plane of Hyrule.
